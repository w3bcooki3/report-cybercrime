What should you do now?

1

Change your passwords

Update your password on the affected services and any other services where you've used the same password.

2

Enable two-factor authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your accounts by enabling 2FA wherever possible.

3

Monitor your accounts

Keep an eye on your accounts for any suspicious activity and report anything unusual immediately.

4

Use a password manager

Consider using a password manager to create and store strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.
