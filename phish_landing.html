RECENT THREATS

Latest Phishing Campaigns

Stay informed about the newest phishing threats that have been identified in the wild.

Alert March 2, 2025

Banking App Security Update Scam

A new wave of SMS messages claiming to be from major banks are circulating, asking users to update their mobile banking app through a provided link. The link leads to a fake app store page.

Read full report →
Alert February 28, 2025

Tax Return Phishing Campaign

With tax season in full swing, fraudsters are sending emails pretending to be from tax authorities, offering "fast refund" services if recipients provide their personal and financial information.

Read full report →
Alert February 25, 2025

Corporate Email Compromise Targeting Executives

A sophisticated spear phishing campaign is targeting C-level executives with fake M&A documents that install keylogging malware when opened. Several Fortune 500 companies have already been affected.

Read full report →
Alert February 20, 2025

Job Offer Phishing Campaign

Fake recruiters are sending detailed job offers with malicious attachments disguised as employment contracts or job descriptions. When opened, these documents deploy credential-stealing malware.

Read full report →
Alert February 15, 2025

Fake Streaming Service Renewal Emails

Users of popular streaming platforms are receiving convincing emails claiming their subscription has expired, directing them to fake payment pages designed to steal credit card information.

Read full report →
Alert February 10, 2025

Healthcare Provider Data Breach Notifications

Phishing emails mimicking data breach notifications from healthcare providers are tricking recipients into clicking malicious links that claim to offer identity protection services.
