LATEST PHISHING TRENDS

Emerging Threat Vectors

AI-Generated Phishing Content

Attackers are increasingly using artificial intelligence to create more convincing phishing content, including personalized messages that mimic writing styles of known contacts and realistic fake websites that are harder to identify as fraudulent.

Multi-Channel Phishing Campaigns

Modern attacks combine multiple channels (email, SMS, voice calls) in coordinated campaigns to increase credibility. For example, a phishing email followed by a confirming text message and then a voice call, all reinforcing the same fraudulent narrative.

Evasion of MFA Through Real-Time Proxies

Advanced attackers now use real-time phishing proxies that can intercept authentication flows, capturing both passwords and multi-factor authentication tokens by acting as a man-in-the-middle between victims and legitimate services.
