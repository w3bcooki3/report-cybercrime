OUR STORY

The People Behind Protect

Meet the team that's driven to make cybersecurity accessible for everyone, regardless of age, gender, background or technical expertise. We're on a mission to create a safer digital world where everyone can navigate online with confidence.

Making Digital Security Accessible to All

Founded in 2022, Protect was born out of a simple yet powerful observation: cybersecurity knowledge and resources were primarily designed for tech-savvy individuals, leaving the majority of internet users vulnerable to evolving digital threats.

We set out to change this by creating a centralized hub where anyone — from children to seniors, from tech novices to professionals — could find the information, tools, and support they need to protect themselves in the digital world.

Today, Protect serves as a trusted advisor to hundreds of thousands of users monthly, offering actionable guidance on recognizing, preventing, and reporting cyber threats. We believe that cybersecurity is a right, not a privilege, and we're committed to bridging the knowledge gap for all internet users.

OUR PRINCIPLES

Mission & Values

The core principles that drive our work and shape our approach to creating a more secure digital world for everyone.

Inclusivity

We design all our resources with accessibility in mind, ensuring that cybersecurity knowledge is available to everyone regardless of technical expertise, age, or background.

Education

We believe in empowering individuals through education rather than fear. Our approach focuses on practical knowledge that builds confidence and develops lasting security habits.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where people can learn from each other's experiences, share insights, and collectively strengthen our digital defenses against evolving threats.

OUR PROGRESS

The Protect Journey

How we've grown from a small educational initiative to become a trusted cybersecurity resource for people around the world.

2022

The Beginning

Founded as a blog focused on providing basic cybersecurity advice for non-technical users and families.

2023

Category Expansion

Developed our comprehensive threat categorization system and released in-depth guides for each category.

2024

Community Growth

Reached 150,000 monthly visitors and launched our interactive reporting system to help users report threats to proper authorities.

2025

Today & Beyond

Continuing to expand our educational resources, tools directory, and community connections to make cybersecurity truly accessible for all.
