Resources & Reporting

Immediate Steps to Take

  • Change your passwords immediately
  • Contact your bank if financial information was shared
  • Disconnect your device from the internet if infected
  • Run an antivirus scan on your device
  • Monitor your accounts for suspicious activity
  • Enable two-factor authentication where possible

Where to Report

  • Report to your email provider
  • Federal Trade Commission (FTC)
  • Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3)
  • Anti-Phishing Working Group
  • Your country's cybersecurity agency
  • Local law enforcement

Prevention Tools

  • Recommended password managers
  • Email filtering solutions
  • Browser security extensions
  • Two-factor authentication apps
  • Security awareness training
  • Security software recommendations
